SEO & Hosting Provider Transfer

Untangle the provider layer without losing the search layer.

This service helps brands move away from old SEO agencies, hosting providers, platform lock-ins, and undocumented website setups. We work through access inventory, SEO preservation, redirect planning, analytics review, DNS coordination, and post-transfer QA.

Transfer checklist

  • Current providers and access holders
  • Registrar, DNS, hosting, and CMS notes
  • Redirect and content migration planning
  • Analytics and Search Console continuity
  • Post-launch QA and owner-facing notes

Use this when

The current setup is unclear, the previous provider is difficult, or a re-platforming project needs someone to protect the visibility and ownership layer while the move happens.

What it prevents

Lost access, broken redirects, missing tracking, provider lock-in surprises, and launches that quietly sacrifice hard-earned search visibility.
