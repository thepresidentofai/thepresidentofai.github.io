Vanity metrics

Vanity metrics

Vanity metrics look impressive in a report but do not clearly connect to business outcomes.

Shortcut Fox Warning

A big number without context can still be a distraction.

Why it matters

Inflated reporting can hide the more useful questions: did the right people visit, did they understand the offer, and did anything meaningful happen next?

What this metric does and does not tell you

Visibility metrics can be useful signals, but they are not the same thing as qualified leads, revenue, or a healthier site structure.
