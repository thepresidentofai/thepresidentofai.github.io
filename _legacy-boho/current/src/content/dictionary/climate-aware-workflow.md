Climate-aware work is about restraint, not perfection cosplay. The practical question is whether the system is lighter, clearer, and more honest than the alternative.

That is why Boho talks about lean websites, measured choices, and support for verified causes rather than inflated claims like zero impact or official neutrality without evidence.
