Metadata is small, but it carries a lot of explanatory weight. It helps define the page's role, especially when multiple pages could look similar from a structural point of view.

Weak metadata does not always cause a visible disaster. More often it contributes to a general loss of clarity, which is why it often gets cleaned up during audits and migrations.
