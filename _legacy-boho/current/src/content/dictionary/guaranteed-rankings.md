shortDefinition: Guaranteed ranking claims promise search outcomes nobody can honestly control.

Search performance is shaped by competition, page quality, structure, trust, and user behavior. Anyone promising fully guaranteed outcomes is flattening that reality into a sales slogan.

Careful providers talk about direction, opportunity, and measurable progress. They do not pretend they own the search engine.
