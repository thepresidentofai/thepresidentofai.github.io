Technical SEO usually becomes visible only when something breaks, but it shapes how much trust a site can earn in the first place.

It includes things like crawl paths, internal linking, canonical logic, redirects, metadata, and the relationship between templates and content. Good technical SEO is rarely glamorous. It is calm, deliberate structure that supports the rest of the site.
