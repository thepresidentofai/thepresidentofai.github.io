Crawlability is about pathways. Search engines need clear routes through the site in the same way people do.

Pages that are buried, duplicated, poorly linked, or structurally confusing tend to lose attention over time. Cleaning up crawlability often means simplifying hierarchy, improving internal links, and removing unnecessary dead ends.
