SEO is not one trick. It is the combined effect of technical health, content clarity, internal structure, trust cues, and the usefulness of the page itself.

When people talk about SEO as if it were only keywords or only backlinks, they usually flatten the system into something easier to sell than to understand. Boho treats it more like habitat maintenance. The goal is to make the right pages discoverable, understandable, and worth choosing.
