DNS is one of those technical layers that stays invisible until a move or outage makes it extremely visible.

For provider transfers, it matters because the domain layer often sits in a different account than the website itself. Good DNS handling means documenting records, timing changes carefully, and knowing what else depends on them before touching anything.
