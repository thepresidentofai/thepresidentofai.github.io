_Last updated_: June 29, 2026

Boho Digital keeps collection light and prefers transparent, low-drama patterns over hidden lead traps.

## What this site does right now

- Shares public studio, service, educational, pricing, and contact information
- Offers a static contact pattern that opens an email draft in your mail client
- Avoids adding background analytics or extra tracking tools unless they are clearly disclosed

## Contact inquiries

If you use the contact page as currently built, the form opens an email draft instead of sending your message through a hidden backend controlled by Boho. That means your message is handled through your own email tool unless the site changes in the future.

## Future changes

If Boho adds analytics, third-party form processing, or other data collection tools later, this page should be updated to reflect what changed and why.
