SEO migration

SEO migration

An SEO migration is the search-protection part of a website move, including URL mapping, redirects, metadata preservation, indexing checks, and Search Console monitoring.

Plain-English version

It is the part of a move that protects your search visibility while the rest of the site changes addresses, structure, or platform.

Why it matters

Without migration planning, even a better new site can lose rankings, traffic, or lead flow simply because the handoff was sloppy.

What to ask your current provider

  • Is there a redirect map?
  • Will metadata be preserved?
  • Who monitors indexing after launch?
