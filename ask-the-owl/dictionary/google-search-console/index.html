Google Search Console

Google Search Console

Google Search Console is a free Google tool that shows how Google sees your website in search, including indexing, queries, clicks, and technical issues.

Plain-English version

It is one of the clearest windows into whether Google can actually see, understand, and trust your site.

Why it matters

Search Console shows indexing issues, search queries, click-through patterns, and crawl problems that ordinary analytics tools do not explain well.

What it does and does not tell you

It helps you understand search visibility. It does not replace full business reporting, conversion tracking, or judgment about what changed and why.
