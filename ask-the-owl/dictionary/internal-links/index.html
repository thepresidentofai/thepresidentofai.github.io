Internal links

Internal links

Internal links are links from one page on your website to another. They help users navigate and help search engines understand structure.

Plain-English version

Internal links are the footpaths that make the forest usable instead of scattered.

Why it matters

Good internal links show which pages matter, connect related topics, and help both humans and crawlers move naturally through the site.

Common mistakes

  • Leaving important pages orphaned
  • Using vague anchor text everywhere
  • Over-linking without structure
