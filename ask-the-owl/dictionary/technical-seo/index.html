Technical SEO

Technical SEO

Technical SEO is the behind-the-scenes work that helps search engines crawl, understand, and index your website.

Back to DictionaryRelated Service

Plain-English version

If your website is a forest, technical SEO is the root system, trail map, and soil health.

Why it matters

Search engines need clear signals before they can show your pages to the right people. Even strong copy can underperform if the site is slow, blocked, duplicated, or hard to follow.

Common issues

  • Pages blocked from indexing
  • Broken links and weak internal paths
  • Duplicate metadata
  • Missing sitemap or schema basics
  • Performance bottlenecks
