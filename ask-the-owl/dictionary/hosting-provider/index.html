Hosting provider

A hosting provider is the company or platform that stores and serves your website files.

Plain-English version

If the registrar holds the address, the host is the land where the house sits.

Why it matters

Hosting affects speed, stability, deployment workflow, and what kind of site is practical to run without unnecessary complexity.

Common questions

  • Who controls the hosting account?
  • Is the site static, CMS-based, or custom?
  • What breaks if the host changes?
