  • Technical SEO review

  • Site structure notes

What moves price
What moves price Why it matters
Market scope A single-city local strategy is different from a national SEO plan across multiple services or regions.
Current site condition A clean static site is easier to improve than a brittle template stack with years of drift.
Content readiness If core service copy, positioning, or proof needs to be shaped, that expands the writing and planning effort.
Access and tooling Missing analytics, DNS, Search Console, CMS, or repository access usually slows everything down.

Hosting note

Included hosting has boundaries.

Free hosting applies to eligible static websites while an active SEO retainer is in place. Domains, email, paid tools, e-commerce, client portals, custom backend systems, and third-party platform fees remain separate costs unless specifically scoped.
