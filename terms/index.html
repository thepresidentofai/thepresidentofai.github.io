Terms

Scope works better when it is named.

These starter terms describe general boundaries for Boho Digital Services. Specific engagements should still be confirmed in a written agreement.

General scope

Boho provides website design, technical SEO, audits, related digital strategy, and selected hosting support based on the agreed scope. Anything outside that scope should be confirmed separately.

Client responsibilities

Clients are responsible for timely access, approvals, source materials, and third-party accounts where required. Delays in access or approvals may affect timelines.

Hosting included with SEO retainers

Eligible static website hosting may be included while an active SEO retainer is in place. Hosting availability, limitations, third-party costs, and transition terms should be confirmed in the applicable service agreement. Domains, email, paid tools, e-commerce, client portals, custom backend systems, and third-party platform fees are not included unless specifically scoped.

No guaranteed outcomes

SEO and website strategy involve variables outside any consultant’s control. Boho Digital Services does not guarantee rankings, traffic, leads, revenue, or specific search results.

Ownership and handoff

Boho aims for client ownership of relevant assets, files, and access wherever possible. Final ownership and transfer details should be defined in the project agreement.

Questions
