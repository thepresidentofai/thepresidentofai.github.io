Privacy

Privacy basics, plainly stated.

This starter privacy page explains how Boho Digital Services may handle inquiry information, basic website analytics, and project communications. It is not legal advice.

What may be collected

Boho may receive the information you choose to send through email, inquiry forms, project documents, analytics tools, and access credentials shared for active work. Typical items include contact details, website URLs, business information, and project notes.

How information is used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, scope work, deliver services, maintain project records, and improve the website. Access and data are handled only as needed to perform the agreed work.

Analytics and third parties

This website may use privacy-conscious analytics or third-party tools for hosting, email, forms, and performance monitoring. Those tools have their own privacy practices.

Client ownership and retention

Project materials, credentials, and client-owned assets should remain under client control where possible. Boho may retain basic project records, invoices, and communications for operational and legal purposes.
