What Boho does

Digital services for businesses that like receipts.

A website should not feel like a locked cabinet. Your SEO strategy should not live in someone else’s dashboard. Boho helps businesses clean up the practical pieces that make a site useful: structure, speed, crawlability, metadata, analytics, local visibility, service pages, content paths, and conversion flow.

Technical SEO

Crawlability, indexation, metadata, internal links, schema, speed basics, and Search Console issues, reviewed and explained in plain English.

Website design

Fast, elegant websites for businesses that need credibility, clarity, search-friendly structure, and ownership without unnecessary platform weight.

Documented strategy

Written roadmaps, implementation notes, cost explanations, access checklists, and next steps you can use after the project ends.

Local or national

Built for local markets and national reach.

Some businesses need to win the map pack down the street. Others need to compete across states, regions, or national search results. The strategy changes, but the standard stays the same: clear structure, useful pages, technical discipline, and honest documentation.

Local market visibility

For businesses that need to stand out in a specific city, region, or service area. Boho helps with website structure, local pages, Google Business Profile alignment, technical SEO, trust signals, and conversion paths.

National service strategy

For service-based businesses competing across broader markets. Boho helps with scalable service pages, content architecture, technical SEO, internal links, metadata, analytics, and long-term search planning.

Not local? Not service-based? Still fine. If the website needs clearer structure, better SEO, faster performance, or a sharper digital presence, Boho can usually help.
