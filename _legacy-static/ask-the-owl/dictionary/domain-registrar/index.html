Domain registrar

A domain registrar is the company where your domain name is registered and managed.

Plain-English version

If your domain is the address, the registrar is the place where that address is officially held.

Why it matters

If you do not know who controls the registrar account, domain renewals, DNS edits, and provider changes can turn into a scramble.

What to ask your current provider

  • Who owns the registrar login?
  • Who receives renewal notices?
  • Can the client access the account directly?
