Climate-aware workflow

Climate-aware workflow

A climate-aware workflow tries to reduce unnecessary digital waste, use tools efficiently, and stay honest about the environmental footprint of digital work.

Plain-English version

It means making digital decisions with less fluff, less waste, and more awareness of the systems underneath the screen.

Why it matters

Digital work still has material costs: storage, compute, transfer, bloated tooling, and unnecessary complexity. Leaner systems are often better for users too.

Where it shows up

Static-first delivery, cleaner asset handling, fewer unnecessary scripts, simpler platforms, and honest reporting about impact.
